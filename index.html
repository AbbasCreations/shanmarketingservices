WE ARE SECOND TO NONE

Shan Marketing Services (SMS) was established in 1994 and has been able to grow every year due to a strong management and sales team. Shan Marketing Services is in the distribution business and has been engrossed in the sales and distribution of various consumer products of multinationals since its inception. Our committed partners include Pepsi Cola, Nestle Pakistan Limited, and Season Edible Oil (Wali Oil Mills). SMS has more than 800 employees and is operating more than 200 commercial vehicles of different capacities to market the products of Nestle, including Nestle F&B, Nestle Waters, Nestle Chilled Dairy, and Nestle Professionals.

F.A.Q

  • Is Shan Marketing Services a marketing company?

    No, Shan Marketing Services is an experienced distribution company that offers support to large manufacturer companies by supplying their products to thousands of retail outlets and corporations.

  • How can I apply for a job at Shan Marketing Services?

    We accept job applications via our listed positions on LinkedIn. You can always submit your resume to hiring@shanmarketing.com to be considered for any open or wanted positions.

  • I have recently opened a new store; how can I start buying products from Shan Marketing Services?

    Email us at info@shanmarketing.com and let us know of your store details. We will setup and activate your account in our system. Once processed we will then be able to start supplying you with the products you need.

  • How many offices does Shan Marketing Services have?

    Our head office is in Sector I-9, Islamabad. We have 7 other offices spread between Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Abbottabad, and Mirpur.

  • What products does Shan Marketing Services distribute?

    We carry Nestle Food and Beverages products, Nestle Waters (including pet and bulk bottles) products, Nestle Yogurt and Chilled Dairy products, Pepsi-Cola products, and Season oil products.
